Scout (ranger) - Masters of the hunt, skilled with ranged weapons and adept at navigating the wilderness. Rangers are versatile adventurers, skilled trackers and survivalists who thrive in the wilderness. They are often protectors of nature, blending martial prowess with a deep connection to the natural world. Equally adept at wielding a bow or blade, rangers are formidable foes, whether hunting beasts or battling those who threaten the balance of the wild. They often have a close bond with animal companions and may possess a touch of magic drawn from nature itself. First names: Alder, Aspen, Briar, Cypress, Elowen, Linden, Rowan, Willow, Fox, Raven, Zephyr Last names: Swiftfoot, Nightshade, Wildwood, Stormblade Hexcraft (warlock/wizard/Witch/sorcerer) - Pact magic users who draw power from otherworldly beings, gaining unique and potent abilities. Masters of arcane lore, specializing in powerful spells and intricate magical theory. Monachos (monk) - Disciplined masters of unarmed combat and ki energy, moving with grace and speed. Rascal (Rogue/Thief) - Skilled in stealth, deception, and trickery, excelling in exploration and combat. Mercenary (warrior/barbarian) - Unbridled rage and raw power, excelling in close-quarters combat. Sentinel/Field warden (fighter) - Skilled warriors who master various weapons and armor, excelling in martial prowess. Bard - Masters of music and magic, inspiring allies and manipulating foes with their artistry. Druid - Guardians of nature, shape-shifting and commanding the forces of the wild. Artificer - Can construct and use gadgets for advantage. Cleric - Divine servants who heal the wounded and smite the wicked, wielding the power of their deity. Paladin - Holy warriors who combine martial might with divine magic to protect the innocent.
